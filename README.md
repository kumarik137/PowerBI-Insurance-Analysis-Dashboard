# Shield Insurance Data Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project was developed as part of a virtual internship assignment. The objective was to transform raw insurance data into meaningful business insights using Power BI. The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer behavior, revenue trends, and policy distribution across different cities and age groups.

## Objectives
- Analyze customer demographics and revenue contribution
- Identify key markets driving insurance revenue
- Understand customer age group distribution
- Present insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Features
The interactive dashboard includes:
- Total Revenue and Total Customer KPIs
- Revenue distribution by city
- Customer segmentation by age group
- Policy performance insights
- Time-based revenue trends

## Key Insights
- Delhi NCR contributes approximately 41% of the total revenue.
- Customers aged 31–40 represent the largest customer segment.
- Revenue generation varies significantly across different cities.
- Certain policy categories show higher customer engagement and contribution.

## Business Impact
The dashboard enables business stakeholders to:
- Identify high-performing regions
- Understand target customer segments
- Support data-driven decision making
- Optimize marketing and policy strategies
